<h3 style="text-align: left;">
  5. BloodMoon
</h3>

* * *

<p style="text-align: left;">
  This plugin will add a BloodMoon event to the server. The chance of BloodMoon each night can be configured.
</p>

[<img class="alignnone wp-image-173 size-full" src="/thinking-minecraft/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/2013-01-28_181831_zpsf84a2c6d.png" alt="2013-01-28_181831_zpsf84a2c6d" width="1600" height="837" srcset="/thinking-minecraft/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/2013-01-28_181831_zpsf84a2c6d.png 1600w, /thinking-minecraft/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/2013-01-28_181831_zpsf84a2c6d-620x324.png 620w, /thinking-minecraft/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/2013-01-28_181831_zpsf84a2c6d-940x491.png 940w, /thinking-minecraft/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/2013-01-28_181831_zpsf84a2c6d-624x326.png 624w" sizes="(max-width: 1600px) 100vw, 1600px" />](/thinking-minecraft/wp-content/uploads/2014/07/2013-01-28_181831_zpsf84a2c6d.png)

During the BloodMoon:

1. Skeletons shoot fire arrows and faster

2. Zombies spawn with weapons and armour

3. Creeper explosions have a chance to cause fire

4. Mobs move faster

5. Hostile mobs killed may respawn as zombies

&nbsp;

A video from the creators illustrating how the plugin works:



Link: <http://dev.bukkit.org/bukkit-plugins/bloodmoon/>

### 4. Factions

* * *

This plugin provides protection for every player from the rest. Factions allows players to create their own factions. the more followers a faction has, the more land it can claim. It can also declare war on enemy factions and claim the land by winning. The risk of grieving is low as only followers can build in the faction.

<img class="alignnone" src="http://dev.bukkit.org/media/images/58/109/logo600.png" alt="" width="554" height="182" />

Link: <http://dev.bukkit.org/bukkit-plugins/factions/>

&nbsp;

### 3. AuthMe Reloaded

* * *

Protect your accounts from grievers with this plugin. This should be a standard plugin on every cracked public server. Every player is forced to use &#8220;/login&#8221; to login to the server every time they join, therefore protecting their accounts with a password.

<img class="alignnone" src="http://www.imagestorming.com/media/cys/1328692322/authme.png" alt="" width="453" height="151" />

Features:

1. Email recovery system

2. Name ban & IP ban

3. Multiple languages available

4. Player name spoof protection

&nbsp;

Link: <http://dev.bukkit.org/bukkit-plugins/authme-reloaded/>
